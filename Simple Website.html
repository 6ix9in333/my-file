Basic ICT Knowledge


What is ICT?

ICT stands for Information and Communication Technology. It involves using computers and other electronic devices to store, retrieve, transmit, and manipulate data or information.

Why is ICT important?

ICT knowledge is essential in today's digital world for communication, accessing information, and carrying out various tasks efficiently.

Basic ICT Concepts

Hardware: Physical components like computers, smartphones, and routers.
Software: Programs and applications that run on hardware.
Internet: A global network connecting computers worldwide.
Data: Information processed and stored by computers.
